Milwaukee Bucks

The Milwaukee Bucks are one of three favourites to win the title this year. The Bucks being led by All-Stars, Giannis Antetokounmpo and Khris Middleton will potentially finish first in the Eastern Conference. Their star player, Giannis Antetokounmpo is having a MVP calibre season once again and I won't be surprised if I see the Bucks in the NBA Finals this year.
